**Limits** are set according to the selected plan, restricting users, projects, clients, and instances. To increase any of these limits, click **"Request Limit"**, enter the desired amount, and submit.

### Limit Types
- **Users**: Maximum number of users allowed in the system.
- **Customer**: Maximum number of customer allowed in the account.
- **Customer Users**: Maximum number of users allowed in the account.
- **Contacts**: Number of contact records of your users.
- **Projects**: Total number of active projects permitted.
- **Instances**: Available system instances for your usage.
