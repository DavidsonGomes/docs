Once your instance is connected to a messaging channel, you can:
- View and manage all messages in the **Chat** screen
- Access and manage contacts in the **Contacts** section
- Interact with users manually through chat
- Use configured bots according to their settings

<Note>If you have configured a bot for your instance, it will follow the stop conditions defined in its settings.</Note>
