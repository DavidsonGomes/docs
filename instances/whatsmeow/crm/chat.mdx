## Messages
The chat interface allows you to interact with both individual contacts and groups. Currently, Evolution CRM supports:
- **WhatsApp messages** - Full chat history and interaction
- **Other channels** (Telegram, Facebook, Instagram) - Not currently displayed in Evolution chat

## Starting a New Chat

To start a conversation with a contact:
1. Click the **+** icon in the top-right corner of the left column
2. Select a contact from your contact list in the modal that opens
3. Begin your conversation
