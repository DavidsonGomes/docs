The Contacts section displays all contacts associated with your connected WhatsApp instance. You can:

- View all contacts from your connected WhatsApp number
- Search contacts using:
  - **remoteJid** - The contact's phone number
  - **pushName** - The contact's name as saved in WhatsApp
- Sort contacts by either field
- Access detailed contact information

<Note>Currently, this feature only shows contacts from WhatsApp channels.</Note>
