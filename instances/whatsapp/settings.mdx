Here, you can manage your instance settings, enabling or disabling specific behaviors:

- **Reject Calls**: Block all incoming calls.
- **Ignore Groups**: Ignore all group messages.
- **Always Online**: Keep WhatsApp always online.
- **Read Messages**: Automatically mark all messages as read.
- **Sync Full History**: Sync the entire chat history when scanning the QR code.
- **Read Status**: Automatically mark all statuses as read.
