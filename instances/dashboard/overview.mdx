title: 'Dashboard'
---

Once created, **access** your **instance** to view a dashboard where you can **update the instance name, delete it**, and track metrics such as the number of contacts, messages, active chats, and the **instance status**. For the **WhatsApp Web channel**, click **"Get QR Code"** to link the customer's contact to your instance within Evolution.
