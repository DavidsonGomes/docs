An instance in Evolution is an **entity** assigned to a **customer within a project**, allowing the selection of a channel **enabled** for that **customer**. It supports integrations with **chatbots**, **events**, or **APIs** using the instance token and ID. For **WhatsApp Web**, simply scan the **QR code** to connect the instance to the customer's WhatsApp.
